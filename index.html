This is an 18+ event. Valid government-issued photo ID is required at the door for entry.

All ticket sales are final. In the event the show is postponed or cancelled, refund instructions will be emailed to all ticket holders.

No — PayPal is the only accepted payment method for this event. Please do not send payment through any other platform.
