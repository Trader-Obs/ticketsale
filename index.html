The Tour

Hunter Mett heads out this fall for a run of intimate club dates, two nights opening for Dermot Kennedy, and an official AmericanaFest showcase in Nashville. Expect a stripped-back, story-driven set built around the songs that got this all started — plus a few that have never been played live before.

The Minneapolis and Milwaukee stops are support dates on Dermot Kennedy's tour, so entry and doors follow the headliner venue's policies. The Montgomery, St. Petersburg, and Tallahassee shows are Hunter's own headline stops with the GA/VIP tiers below. Ages and entry requirements vary by venue — check the venue listing for each city.

Good to Know

How do I receive my ticket?

After your PayPal payment is confirmed, an e-ticket / entry confirmation will be emailed to you within 24 hours. Bring a photo ID that matches the name on your PayPal payment note.

Is this show all ages?

This is an 18+ event. Valid government-issued photo ID is required at the door for entry.

What's your refund policy?

All ticket sales are final. In the event the show is postponed or cancelled, refund instructions will be emailed to all ticket holders.

Can I pay another way?

No. PayPal is the only accepted payment method for tickets and the Fan Card. Please do not send payment through any other platform.

What do I get with the Fan Card?

Use the Fan Card builder above to pick your tier, enter your name, and complete payment via PayPal — the note field there gives you a ready-made line to paste into your PayPal payment. Your digital card arrives by email within 24 hours; the physical card ships to the mailing address you include in that note.
